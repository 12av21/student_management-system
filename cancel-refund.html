Rules of Refund

Refund will be made as per latest government advisory within 30 working days from the date of application. For refund of fees, the candidate must apply on white paper in Registrar office providing the following information: Name of candidate, Fathers name, College Enrolment number, Roll number, Course in which admission has been taken, Fees amount along with copy of fee receipt, Copy of Aadhar card, Account details in which fee will be refunded (Beneficiary name Beneficiary account number, Name of Bank and Branch & IFSC code). Fee will be refunded only electronically.
